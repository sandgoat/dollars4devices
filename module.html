Does your phone have a bad ESN/IMEI?

A bad ESN/IMEI can be a result of many factors, including but not limited to, a phone currently being active on an account, reported Lost or Stolen, or an unpaid balance due on the account.

You can check the ESN/IMEI Status of your phone by visiting www.CheckESNFree.com.

If you're unsure on the Finance status of your phone, you can check by calling your carrier.

Please also be advised that we do not purchase phones have been reported Lost/Stolen or ones that are Activation / iCloud Locked.
