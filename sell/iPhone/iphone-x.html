FAQs

› What are the common iPhone X trade-in issues?

Typically with newer iPhones, we do not see a lot of hardware issues. However, we've seen a very small percentage of iPhone X devices come in with faulty Face ID hardware.

Additionally, many iPhone X devices are still under a Finance plan. To receive the full trade-in value when you sell your iPhone X, any unpaid balances would need to be paid off.

› When's the best time to sell my iPhone X?

As of December, 2018. The iPhone X has lost about 30%* of its trade-in value in one year. The best time to sell iPhone X would be as soon as you have a replacement device as prices rarely trend upwards over time.


* Based on the trade-in value of an iPhone X AT&T 64GB in Flawless condition.

› What steps should I take before selling my iPhone X?
  1. Backup your iPhone X to your computer or use Apple's iCloud Backup Service. This way all your data can be transferred over to your new device.
  2. Erase your iPhone X and be sure to to confirm Find my iPhone has been turned off.
  3. If you're on a CDMA network such as Verizon / Sprint, you may need to call them to deactivate your line from the device.
  4. Remove any cases / screen protectors from the device before you assess the condition. Often times, scratching / damage is hidden underneath.
  5. Remove your SIM Card. You may need it to keep your existing number and service.
› Should you sell your iPhone X and upgrade?

The iPhone X is only a year old, has most of the latest features, and is still quite powerful. For most users there isn't a strong compelling reason to upgrade.

From a performance standpoint, there is a slight improvement, however, most users will not notice much of a difference. It might be worthwhile for hardcore gamers though. In addition, the added 512GB storage option may be compelling enough to upgrade.

In terms of features, the only feature lacking on the iPhone X is the dual-sim support. For individuals who need multiple lines for business or travel needs, an upgrade may be worthwhile.

Finally, a big reason to upgrade is the extra screen size on the iPhone XS Max. If screen size is important to you, then it may be worthwhile.

Should you decide to make the switch, you can trade in your iPhone with us.
